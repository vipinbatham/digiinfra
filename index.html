How Our Technology Works

A seamless integration of AI, computer vision, and real-time notification systems

1

Road Imaging & Data Collection

High-resolution cameras mounted on HNAI inspection vehicles capture detailed road surface images across the national highway network. Our system processes thousands of images per hour, covering extensive road segments with millimeter-level precision.

2

AI-Powered Defect Analysis

Our proprietary computer vision models analyze captured images to detect and classify various road defects including potholes, cracks, rutting, surface deterioration, and pavement markings. The system uses deep learning algorithms trained on over 2 million annotated road images.

3

Real-time Notification & Reporting

Immediate alerts are automatically sent to HNAI maintenance teams with defect location (GPS coordinates), severity classification, recommended repair priority, and estimated repair time. The system integrates with HNAI's existing workflow management tools.

Specialized AI Models for Road Defects

Our computer vision models are specifically trained and optimized for Indian road conditions:

  • Pothole Detection Model: Detects potholes from 5cm to 2m diameter with depth estimation
  • Crack Classification Model: Identifies transverse, longitudinal, and alligator cracks with severity grading
  • Surface Condition Model: Assesses raveling, bleeding, polishing, and other surface defects
  • Pavement Marking Model: Evaluates visibility and condition of road markings and signage
  • Multi-defect Model: Simultaneously detects and classifies multiple defect types in a single image

Training Data: Our models are trained on over 2 million annotated images from Indian highways across different seasons, weather conditions, and times of day.
